To begin generating videos, move on tabs and follow steps:

  1. Products: register your products assets (images and texts)
  2. Bases: upload a base video and define start/end moments to each product
  3. Offer Types: tell us which elements should be displayed on screen
  4. Preview: finally, generate a preview video uploaded to Drive using product, base and offer type
  5. Video Ads: Ready to go live? Same as preview but uploaded to YouTube
  6. After upload to YouTube, your AdsScript will automatically setup Video Ads inside Google Ads account
